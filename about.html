ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Vivian!

I was born and raised in Canada. Given this, I have always been exposed to many different interesting cultures, especially in Toronto. This constant exposure has fostered my curiosity towards other people of the world and my drive to learn more.

Me terrorizing the local art gallery.

In my free time, you would most likely find me drawing, doing crafts, coding, playing video games or even picking up a new online course. I like to constantly solve problems, stimulate creativity and most importantly learn!

I remember the beginning of my interest in travelling to be in my World Religions course at school. My teacher covered Native Spirituality, Judaism, Islam, Christianity, Hinduism and Buddhism—so many religions! Each of them each had their own unique beliefs, customs and communities. The course really opened my eyes about the sheer diversity of the world and gave me a taste of a few of their rich cultures. World Religions was the spark—and I am so grateful for it.

Going to New York.

I love to travel and created this blog to share all my intriguing experiences around the world to everybody, documenting the diverse world of religion on the side. It is a great opportunity for me to learn more about others and connect with them, something that has been largely overlooked in the modern world today.

Let's go!
